Showcase Your Humor!

Get ready to tickle funny bones and leave the audience in splits at the Standup Comedy competition! This is your chance to shine with your comedic talent in an individual competition.   • The time limit is 3 minutes
  • Participants will be judged based on content, fluency, spontaneity, presentation, and sense of humor
