Shipwreck is a catastrophic event where a ship sinks or is stranded, typically resulting in loss of life, damage to cargo, and environmental impact. Shipwrecks can occur due to various reasons such as storms, navigational errors, equipment failure, or collisions with other vessels or natural obstacles like rocks or icebergs. They have been a significant part of maritime history, often leading to stories of survival, exploration, and archaeological discoveries.
