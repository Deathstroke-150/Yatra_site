Ready, Set, Eat!

Join us for the ultimate eating challenge at Eat 'O Mania! This solo event is all about speed and stomach capacity. Participants will compete to see who can consume the provided food items the fastest. With the menu being revealed on the spot, be prepared for anything! The judge's decision will determine the winner, so give it your all!

Eat 'O Mania" is a game where you eat as much food as you can. Various kinds of food would be provided, and you earn points for each food you eat. The game gets harder as you go, and you have to hit the score within the allotted time to win the game!

Prizes and Rewards

Winner - ₹1000

Runner - ₹750

Rules and Regulations

  • It is a solo event
  • The fastest to eat will be the winner
  • Any food items will be provided on the spot
  • The judge's decision will be the final
