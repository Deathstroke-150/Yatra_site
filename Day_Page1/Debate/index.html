Debate (English)

₹75

Max registrations - 20

Engage in lively discussions and exchange of ideas in the English Debate!

Participate in the English Debate and showcase your oratory and argumentation skills! Whether you're passionate about public speaking or want to refine your debating skills, this is your chance to shine. Join a team of three members and engage in thought-provoking discussions on various topics.

Participants engage in quick-witted exchanges, voicing their opinions without the need for a rigid logical structure or exhaustive evidence. With a team of 3, the stage is set for a rapid-fire exchange of ideas, where staying composed is the key to success.

Prizes and Rewards

Winner - ₹1000

Runner - ₹500

Rules and Regulations

  • Team Composition: Each team must have 3 members.
  • Time Limits: 10 mins for a topic.
  • Scoring: The judge's decision will be final.
  • Language Use: Participants must use English language only.
  • Research: Participants can use their mobiles to take points on the topic given.
